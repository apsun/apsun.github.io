Hello! I graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a B.S. in Computer Science. My main interests are systems programming, networking, and security. I currently work at Facebook as a Production Engineer.

Projects

  • loliOS (C, x86 assembly)

    A small and lightweight Linux-like kernel, implemented from scratch. Supports signals, audio, networking, fork/exec, pipes, and more. Includes a basic userspace environment that can connect to internet servers and play music, amongst other things. Originally a project for the Computer Systems Engineering course at UIUC.

  • uSticker (Kotlin, Android)

    An extension for the Android Gboard app that lets you use any image from your device as a sticker. You can find it on Google Play here.

  • AbsoluteTouchEx (C++, Windows)

    Lets you use your Windows precision touchpad as a tablet in games. Simulates cursor input by injecting itself into the target process and intercepting touchpad HID events. Replaces the older AbsoluteTouch project, which only worked with Synaptics touchpads.

  • NekoSMS (Java, Android)

    A pattern-based SMS blocker for Android, currently the only standalone SMS blocker that supports Android 4.4 and above. Requires Xposed framework. Motivated the creation of RemotePreferences, a library for accessing Android preferences across app boundaries.

  • SoundCloud to MP3 (JavaScript)

    Firefox add-on that adds a download button to SoundCloud tracks. Doesn't call out to any external services, and supports downloading in AAC format if you have a Go+ subscription. You can download it here.

  • GoogleImageShell (C#, Windows)

    Upload images to Google reverse image search directly from the Windows Explorer context menu.

  • QuoteLock (Java, Android)

    Originally developed as a silly frontend for Visual Novel as a Service, this app displays random quotes on your Android lockscreen. Requires Xposed framework.
